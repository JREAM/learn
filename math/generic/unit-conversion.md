# Unit Conversion
This could be handy.

## Computer Bits
The kilo notation used is 1024.

    - 1 bit: 1
        - Smallest Unit
        - 8-bits = 1 byte
    - 1 byte(b): 8 bits
    - 1 kilobyte(kb): 1024 b
    - 1 megabyte(mb): 1024 kb
    - 1 gigabyte(gb): 1024 mb
    - 1 terabyte(tb): 1024 gb
    - 1 petabyte(pb): 1024 tb

    - Nanosecond:
        - Seconds: 0.000000001
    - Microsecond:
        - Seconds: 0.000001
    - Millisecond:
        - Seconds: 0.001
    - Seconds:
        - Milliseconds: 1000
            - `1 second = ms * 1000`
    - Minute:
        - Seconds: 60
            - `1 minute = sec * 60`
    - Hour:
        - Seconds: 3600
            - `hour = (sec * 60) / 60`
        - Minutes: 60
            - `hour = min * 60`
    - Day:
        - Seconds: 86400
        - Minutes: 1440
        - Hours: 24
